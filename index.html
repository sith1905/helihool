Music for those who need it most.
We make high-quality live music available where it is needed the most: hospitals, schools, public gardens, offices and other places.

Donate

Our Goal

Our main goal is to attract talented musicians towards those who need music (hospice patients, residents of nursing homes, war refugees, kids) by organising several concerts per month, providing them with an opportunity to lead a decent cultural and social life.

We encourage young musicians to create interesting concert programs that can be used outside of their academic institutions, and become a solid base for their professional career development.

We strive to bring community music to a level that can be appreciated and supported by the governmental organisations and various communities here in Estonia.
